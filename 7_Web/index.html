Introduction

Time magazine, 2019 March 1: “Measles Cases Are Increasing Worldwide Amid 'Mistrust and Complacency,' U.N. Warns"
Measles cases are soaring around the world, including in areas previously declared measles free, according to the U.N. Children’s Fund. Last year, 98 countries reported an uptick of measles cases, UNICEF said Thursday, while pinning the “alarming” resurgence of the disease on poor infrastructure and conflict in some cases, and “complacency” and an anti-vaccine movement in others. “Almost all of these cases are preventable, and yet children are getting infected even in places where there is simply no excuse,” said Henrietta H. Fore, UNICEF’s executive director.

In a world where we have the technology and knowledge to alleviate and conquer these ills, these once-irradicated diseases are on the rise and are threatening with serious consequences. As society has embraced self-determination, has it also lost the wisdom of altruism? Is it misconception or misrepresentation?

Purpose

The purpose of this project was to demonstrate interactive visualization in a practical application. Infectious Diseases data in the US was perfect subject to provide meaningful visualization on an issue that has serious consequences in the world today. The Poxy_Server team hoped to further understand how well-known diseases infect the US landscape and how effective are vaccinations efforts.
